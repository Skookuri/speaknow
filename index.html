Break down communication barriers with SpeakNow. Communicate with confidence, express your thoughts, and connect with your world.



From stroke-induced aphasia to selective mutism, an estimated 18.5 million individuals in the US face the challenges of speech, voice, or language disorders. These conditions span across all age groups, presenting formidable barriers in daily life where communication, consent, and self-expression play pivotal roles.

Moreover, existing assistive technologies often fall short in terms of user-friendliness and customization options, failing to adequately serve those who require them.
At SpeakNow, our mission is to provide a solution that prioritizes customization and adaptability, with the ultimate goal of empowering individuals in need with a voice of their own. By offering a service that embraces flexibility, we aspire to create a more accessible world where everyone can communicate confidently and authentically.
